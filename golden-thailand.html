Golden Thailand 2.0 2026

5 Days / 4 Nights  ·  Pattaya + Bangkok  ·  via Cebu Pacific  ·  from ₱32,888

Package Overview

Two of Thailand's most loved cities — Pattaya and Bangkok — combined into one well-paced 5-day itinerary. From elephant shows and the Sanctuary of Truth on the Pattaya coast, to Wat Arun, ICONSIAM, and a magical dinner cruise on the Chao Phraya in the heart of Bangkok, this package gives you the full Thailand experience without the planning headache.

Stay 1 night at the 5-star Brighton Grand Hotel (or similar) in Pattaya and 2 nights at the 4-star Pure Eleven Hotel (or similar) in Bangkok. Includes high-valued meals, private coach, fully loaded tours, an English-speaking local guide, and a dedicated Filipino tour escort.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Lunch & Dinner

Manila → Bangkok → Pattaya (Nong Nooch & Cultural Show)

  • Departure from Manila via Cebu Pacific
  • Arrival in Bangkok, transfer to Pattaya
  • Visit Nong Nooch Village with Tram Riding
  • Enjoy Thai Cultural Show & Elephant Show
  • Visit Chocolate Factory
  • Hotel check-in at Brighton Grand Hotel (5-Star) or similar
Day 2 Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Pattaya → Bangkok (Sanctuary of Truth, Wat Arun, ICONSIAM, Dinner Cruise)

  • Visit Gems Gallery with Lights and Sound Show
  • Visit Sanctuary of Truth — Pattaya's iconic all-wood seaside temple
  • Transfer to Bangkok
  • Visit Wat Arun Temple (Temple of Dawn)
  • Visit ICONSIAM
  • Enjoy Dinner Cruise on the Chao Phraya River
  • Hotel check-in at Pure Eleven Hotel (4-Star) or similar
Day 3 Breakfast

Bangkok — Free Day

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Enjoy your free day — explore Pratunam area and other nearby stores around your hotel
  • Optional add-on tours available on request (e.g. floating market, Damnoen Saduak, Maeklong Railway Market, Ayutthaya)
Day 4 Breakfast

Bangkok (Siam Premium Outlet, Wat Saket, Asiatique)

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Enjoy free time in the morning
  • Visit Siam Premium Outlet
  • Visit Wat Saket Temple (Golden Mount)
  • Shop at Asiatique The Riverfront night market
  • Transfer to airport
Day 5 No meals

Bangkok → Manila

  • Arrival in Manila
