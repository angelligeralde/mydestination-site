Go El Nido 2026

4 Days / 3 Nights  ·  via Cebu Pacific  ·  Includes 2 Island-Hopping Tours  ·  from ₱14,888

Package Overview

Limestone karsts rising from emerald lagoons, hidden beaches reached only by boat, and some of the most breathtaking island-hopping in the world — El Nido, Palawan is consistently ranked among the best islands on the planet.

This 4-day, 3-night package flies you to Puerto Princesa via Cebu Pacific, then transfers you onward to El Nido by van. The headline experience: two full island-hopping tours — the famous Tour A (lagoons & beaches) and Tour C (hidden coves & secret beach) — both included in the package price.

Day 1 No meals

Manila → Puerto Princesa → El Nido

  • Arrival at Puerto Princesa Airport
  • Van transfer to El Nido (approx. 5–6 hours)
  • Hotel check-in & rest
Day 2 Breakfast

Tour A — Island Hopping

Visit the following:

  • Big or Small Lagoon
  • Shimizu Island
  • Seven Commando Beach
  • Payong-Payong Beach
  • Secret Lagoon
Day 3 Breakfast

Tour C — Island Hopping

Visit the following:

  • Helicopter Island
  • Hidden Beach
  • Talisay Beach
  • Matinloc Shrine (with snorkeling)
  • Secret Beach
Day 4 Breakfast

Departure Day

  • Breakfast at the hotel & free time
  • Hotel check-out
  • Van transfer back to Puerto Princesa Airport
  • Flight back to Manila
