Go Iloilo & Guimaras 2026

4 Days / 3 Nights  ·  via Cebu Pacific  ·  Island Hopping + City Tour  ·  from ₱15,888

Package Overview

Two destinations, one unforgettable trip. This 4-day, 3-night package pairs the sweet mango island of Guimaras — with its quiet beaches, hidden coves, and Trappist Monastery — with the food-loving, history-rich city of Iloilo, home to centuries-old churches, ancestral mansions, and the famous La Paz batchoy.

Fly via Cebu Pacific from Manila to Iloilo, then cross to Guimaras by pumpboat for two nights at a beach resort, an island-hopping tour, and a taste of the world-famous Guimaras mango. Return to Iloilo City for a heritage day tour through Molo, Jaro, Garin Farm, and the museum-lined Calle Real.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Lunch & Dinner

Arrival in Iloilo → Guimaras Land Tour

  • Departure from Manila → arrival at Iloilo International Airport
  • Airport pick-up
  • Breakfast at La Paz Batchoy (own account)
  • Transfer to Parola Wharf → boat to Jordan Wharf, Guimaras
  • Visit San Lorenzo Wind Farm Project
  • Visit Guimaras Landmark
  • Lunch at a local seafood restaurant
  • Visit Trappist Monastery
  • Proceed to beach resort for check-in & free time for swimming
  • Dinner & overnight at the resort

Optional stops (own account): Mango Market & Grocery, Olivia's Kitchen for mango pizza, mango shake, mango coffee.

Day 2 Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Guimaras Island Hopping

  • Breakfast at the resort
  • Enjoy a 2–3 hour Island Hopping Tour — visit:
    • Natago Beach Resort
    • La Murawan
    • Baras Cave
    • Ave Maria Islet
  • Shower & lunch at the resort
  • Free time & swimming
  • Dinner & overnight at the resort
Day 3 Breakfast

Rest Day → Transfer to Iloilo City

  • Breakfast at the resort & free time
  • Check-out, lunch on own account
  • Transfer: resort → Jordan Wharf → boat to Parola Wharf → Iloilo hotel
  • Hotel check-in
  • Dinner on own account & overnight in Iloilo
Day 4 Breakfast

Iloilo City Tour → Garin Farm → Departure

  • Breakfast at the hotel & check-out
  • Visit Garin Farm
  • Visit Miag-ao Church (UNESCO World Heritage)
  • Visit Molo Mansion & Molo Church
  • Festive Walk to the Brandy Museum
  • Calle Real Rolling Tour — Philippine Maritime Museum, Iloilo Economic History Museum, Iloilo Prison Jail Museum
  • Visit Biscocho Haus for pasalubong
  • Visit Jaro Cathedral Church
  • Transfer to Iloilo International Airport & departure for Manila

Optional: early dinner at Tatoy's Airport Branch (own account).

The schedule may be interchanged based on your flight details, weather conditions, and unforeseen circumstances — but all indicated destinations will still be visited.
