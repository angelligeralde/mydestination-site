Go Boracay 2026

4 Days / 3 Nights  ·  via Cebu Pacific (Caticlan)  ·  5 Hotel Options  ·  from ₱12,888

Package Overview

White sand, turquoise water, and the most famous island sunset in the Philippines — Boracay is the country's premier beach destination, and this 4-day, 3-night package gets you there with zero hassle.

Fly via Cebu Pacific from Manila to Caticlan (the closest airport to Boracay), and choose from five hand-picked hotels across Station 1, Station 2, Station 3, and Newcoast — from beachfront 4-star comfort to relaxed budget-friendly options. Free time throughout your stay means you can dive into the water, book island-hopping on your own, or simply unwind.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 No meals

Manila → Caticlan → Boracay

  • Departure from Manila via Cebu Pacific
  • Arrival at Caticlan Airport (MPH)
  • Meet & greet, transfer to Boracay via private boat & van
  • Hotel check-in & free time on the beach
Day 2 Breakfast

Boracay — Free Time

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Free day — swim, walk White Beach, optional island hopping, Puka Beach, parasailing, sunset cruise (optional add-ons)
Day 3 Breakfast

Boracay — Free Time

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Another full free day to enjoy the island at your own pace
Day 4 Breakfast

Departure Day

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Hotel check-out
  • Transfer to Caticlan Airport for return flight to Manila
