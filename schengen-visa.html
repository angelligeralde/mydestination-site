Visa Consultancy

Expert Schengen Tourist Visa Assistance

Planning a trip across Europe? We help you apply through the right embassy and prepare a strong Schengen Visa application.

Your Trusted Partner for Schengen Visa Applications

The Schengen Visa allows you to travel freely across 27 European countries with a single visa — from France and Italy to Germany, Spain, the Netherlands, Greece, and beyond. But the application must be filed at the embassy of your main destination, and the requirements can vary slightly by country.

That's where we come in.

Our team helps you determine the correct embassy for your application, prepares your documents, and guides you through appointment scheduling and the interview process. Many Schengen embassies process applications through visa application centers (VFS Global, BLS, or TLScontact).

Whether you're visiting one country or planning a multi-country itinerary, we'll make sure your application gives you the strongest chance of approval.

Processing Time: Visa processing times vary by embassy/consulate and season. Please contact our agents for the most current processing time estimate for your application.

Our Schengen Visa Assistance Service

We assist you throughout the key steps of your Schengen visa application process.

1

Embassy Determination & Form Assistance

The first step is identifying which embassy you should apply to — usually the country where you'll spend the most time.

We will:

  • Determine the correct embassy for your itinerary
  • Complete the Schengen Visa application form
  • Ensure all information is consistent with your travel plans
  • Prepare your application packet for submission
2

Document Preparation & Review

Schengen embassies are strict on documentation: travel insurance with min. €30,000 coverage, financial proof, itinerary, and accommodation are all required.

We will:

  • Provide a complete country-specific document checklist
  • Review your bank statements, ITR, and employment proof
  • Help build a credible day-by-day itinerary
  • Verify your travel insurance meets the €30,000 requirement
3

Appointment & Submission

Most Schengen embassies require an in-person appointment at a visa application center for biometrics submission.

We will:

  • Schedule your VFS / BLS / TLS appointment
  • Brief you on the submission day requirements
  • Coordinate biometrics capture
  • Prepare you for any interview questions
4

Ongoing Support & Questions

We support you from initial inquiry through visa release.

We will:

  • Answer all your questions about Schengen rules
  • Guide multi-country itinerary planning
  • Help with sponsorship and invitation letters
  • Track your application until passport release

Who Should Use Our Visa Assistance Service?

  • First-time Schengen visa applicants
  • Travelers who want guidance through the process
  • Applicants who want to avoid mistakes in their forms
  • Anyone who wants extra preparation and document review
