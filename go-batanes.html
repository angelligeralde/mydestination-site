Go Batanes 2026

4 Days / 3 Nights  ·  via Philippine Airlines  ·  Departing Clark  ·  All-In Package

Departure from Clark International Airport (CRK). In line with government directives limiting turboprop operations at Manila's NAIA, all flights to Batanes depart from Clark International Airport (CRK) via Philippine Airlines.

Package Overview

Experience the windswept beauty of the northernmost tip of the Philippines — Batanes. This fully-loaded 4-day, 3-night package takes you through the rugged landscapes of Batan Island, the traditional stone villages of Sabtang Island, and the rolling hills and dramatic coastlines that make Batanes unlike anywhere else in the country.

Fly via Philippine Airlines departing from Clark International Airport (CRK), travel with a licensed tour guide, and stay at Villa Hontomin or Bernardo's Lantia Hotel — all at one all-in price with no hidden charges.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Breakfast & Lunch

Arrival & Batan North Tour

  • Departure from Clark International Airport (CRK) — arrival in Basco, Batanes
  • Meet & greet at Basco Airport, transfer to hotel & check-in
  • Breakfast & free time to relax

Batan North Tour — visit the following:

  • Valugan Boulder Bay
  • Japanese Tunnel
  • Basco Cathedral
  • Tukon Chapel
  • Basco Lighthouse
  • Basco Rolling Hills
  • Radar Station
  • Casa Real
  • Amandangat
  • Kilometer Zero
Day 2 Breakfast & Lunch

Sabtang Island Tour

  • Proceed to Sabtang Island via Faluwa (traditional boat)
  • Transfer to Ivana Port
  • Transfer to hotel & free time

Sabtang Island Tour — visit the following:

  • San Vicente Ferrer Church
  • Savidug Community
  • Savidug Idjang Fortress
  • Tinyan Viewing of Pacific Ocean
  • Vernacular Houses
  • Chavayan Community Vernacular Houses
  • Ahaw Arc
  • Morong Beach
  • Souvenir Shops
Day 3 Breakfast & Lunch

Batan South Tour

Visit the following:

  • Racuh A Payaman
  • Mahatao Church Complex
  • Chawa View Deck
  • Mahatao Shelter Port
  • Maydangeb White Beach (no swimming)
  • San Jose Spanish Bridge
  • House of Dakay
  • Honesty Coffee Shop
  • Ivana Church Complex
  • Mutchong Viewpoint
  • Itbud Church
  • Madangay Hills & Alapad Rock Formation
  • BAMSO Museum
  • Transfer to hotel
Day 4 Breakfast

Departure Day

  • Departure for Manila
