Tour Packages FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Tour Packages

Quick answers about booking, payment, customization, and what's included in our packages.

How do I book a tour package?
Click the 'Inquire About This Package' button on any package page to send us a quick inquiry. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours with full details, current pricing, availability, and the next steps to confirm your booking.
What's typically included in your tour packages?
Most of our packages include flights, accommodation, airport transfers, daily tours with English-speaking guides, and select meals. Each package has a clear inclusions list — check the package page or ask our team for specifics.
What is your payment policy?
For most packages, full payment is required 60 days before travel date. A booking deposit may be required to secure your slot. Rates and fuel surcharges are subject to change without prior notice. See each package's payment section for specifics.
Can I customize an existing package?
Yes. While we publish set packages, we are happy to customize the itinerary, dates, hotel category, group size, and inclusions to match your preferences and budget. Just tell us what you'd like to adjust.
Do you offer both domestic and international packages?
Yes. We offer domestic packages to popular destinations like Boracay, Palawan (El Nido, Coron), Siargao, Batanes, Iloilo & Guimaras, plus international packages to Japan, Korea, Vietnam, Taiwan, Bali, Singapore, Dubai, Turkey, Europe, and more.
Can I book just flights or hotels without the full package?
Yes. We offer standalone services for flights, hotels, transfers, travel insurance, and visa assistance. You don't need to book a full package — let us know what you need.
