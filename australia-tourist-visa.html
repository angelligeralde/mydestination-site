Visa Consultancy

Expert Australia Tourist Visa Assistance

From Sydney Harbour to the Great Barrier Reef — let us prepare your Australia Tourist Visa (subclass 600) application with confidence.

Your Trusted Partner for Australia Visa Applications

Australia is a top destination for Filipino travelers visiting family, exploring iconic cities, or experiencing the country's natural wonders. Most Filipino passport holders apply for the Visitor Visa (subclass 600) through the online ImmiAccount system.

That's where we come in.

Our team manages your entire Australia Tourist Visa application — from ImmiAccount setup to document upload and final submission. The Australian government takes a strict view on genuine temporary stay, so the supporting documents and statements must clearly demonstrate your intent to return to the Philippines.

Whether you're applying as an independent tourist or being sponsored by family, we'll prepare a compelling application.

Processing Time: Visa processing times vary by embassy/consulate and season. Please contact our agents for the most current processing time estimate for your application.

Our Australia Visa Assistance Service

We assist you throughout the key steps of your Australia visa application process.

1

Application Form Assistance

We help you set up your ImmiAccount and complete the subclass 600 application with accuracy.

We will:

  • Create and manage your ImmiAccount
  • Complete the Visitor Visa application form
  • Ensure consistency across all answers
  • Submit the application correctly
2

Document Preparation & Review

Australia evaluates genuine visitor intent carefully — financial capacity, ties to home, and travel purpose must all be clearly evidenced.

We will:

  • Provide the complete document checklist
  • Review bank statements, ITR, and employment records
  • Help craft your personal statement / letter of intent
  • Coordinate sponsor documents if applicable
3

Submission & Biometrics

Some applicants may be requested to provide biometrics at the Australian Visa Application Centre (AVAC).

We will:

  • Submit the application through ImmiAccount
  • Coordinate biometrics appointment if requested
  • Track the status of your application
  • Respond to any document requests from the Department
4

Ongoing Support & Questions

We support you all the way through to visa grant.

We will:

  • Answer questions on stay duration and multi-entry
  • Guide first-time vs. returning applicants
  • Help respond to Department requests for more info
  • Support you up to your travel date

Who Should Use Our Visa Assistance Service?

  • First-time Australia visa applicants
  • Travelers who want guidance through the process
  • Applicants who want to avoid mistakes in their forms
  • Anyone who wants extra preparation and document review
