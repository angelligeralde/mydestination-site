Who We Are

A Premier Travel Agency Built on Passion and Purpose

My Destination Tours and Travel is a premier travel agency committed to providing high-quality travel services to clients all over the world. We are dedicated to creating exceptional travel experiences with an emphasis on personalized attention, customer satisfaction, and unique travel itineraries.

We specialize in customizing travel packages to meet our clients' individual needs, interests, and budget. Our team of experienced travel professionals has in-depth knowledge of various travel destinations — their cultures, attractions, and local customs.

Our goal is to make travel accessible to everyone, regardless of background, budget, or travel experience. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment that embraces diversity, cultural exchange, and responsible tourism.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

Our mission is tied to our tagline — "Your Destination. My Destination." We treat our clients' destination as our own personal destination. We provide expert services to make travel hassle-free — from the first inquiry to the moment clients return home. Our responsibility doesn't end after booking. Even during travel, we ensure every planned itinerary runs smoothly, nurturing a family-like relationship with our clients.

Our Vision

We want My Destination to be seen as the most sought-after travel agency and destination management company in the Philippines — doing business locally and internationally, connecting clients and partners across the industry in a prosperous and mutually beneficial cycle.

Our Values

Honesty and integrity in all of our dealings with clients and suppliers, while keeping a simplistic approach to doing business. We believe in win-win relationships — every partnership we form benefits both parties and contributes to a thriving travel community for everyone involved.
