Visa Consultancy

Expert New Zealand Tourist Visa Assistance

From the fjords of the South Island to the geothermal wonders of Rotorua — let us prepare your New Zealand Visitor Visa application.

Your Trusted Partner for New Zealand Visa Applications

New Zealand is a dream destination for Filipino travelers chasing dramatic landscapes, adventure sports, and warm Kiwi hospitality. Filipino passport holders typically need a Visitor Visa to enter New Zealand, applied for online through the Immigration New Zealand portal.

That's where we come in.

Our team handles the entire process — from online account setup to completing your application, preparing supporting documents, and tracking until your visa is approved.

Whether you're visiting family, going on a holiday, or attending a special event, we'll make sure your application meets Immigration New Zealand's standards.

Processing Time: Visa processing times vary by embassy/consulate and season. Please contact our agents for the most current processing time estimate for your application.

Our New Zealand Visa Assistance Service

We assist you throughout the key steps of your New Zealand visa application process.

1

Application Form Assistance

We help you set up your Immigration NZ online account and complete the Visitor Visa application.

We will:

  • Create and manage your Immigration NZ account
  • Complete the Visitor Visa application accurately
  • Ensure consistency across your supporting documents
  • Submit the application properly
2

Document Preparation & Review

New Zealand requires strong evidence of your genuine intent to visit and ability to fund your trip.

We will:

  • Provide a complete document checklist
  • Review bank statements, ITR, and employment records
  • Help craft your personal statement / cover letter
  • Coordinate sponsor documents if applicable
3

Submission & Health Requirements

Some applicants may need to provide medical certificates or police clearance for longer stays.

We will:

  • Submit your application through INZ
  • Advise on medical or police clearance requirements
  • Coordinate any additional document requests
  • Track approval and final decision
4

Ongoing Support & Questions

We stay with you from inquiry through approval.

We will:

  • Answer questions about NZeTA vs. Visitor Visa
  • Clarify stay duration and entry rules
  • Guide multi-entry and longer stays
  • Support you up to your travel date

Who Should Use Our Visa Assistance Service?

  • First-time New Zealand visa applicants
  • Travelers who want guidance through the process
  • Applicants who want to avoid mistakes in their forms
  • Anyone who wants extra preparation and document review
