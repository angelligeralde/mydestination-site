First Love Hong Kong-Macau 2026

4 Days / 3 Nights  ·  via Cebu Pacific  ·  Cozi Oasis (4-Star)  ·  from $555

Package Overview

Two iconic Asian destinations, one easy package. This 4-day, 3-night trip pairs Hong Kong's skyline, harbor, and culture with a full-day visit to Macau's UNESCO-listed historic centre and the dazzling Venetian Macao.

You'll stay 3 nights at the Cozi Oasis (4-Star), get a guided half-day Hong Kong city tour on arrival, two free days to explore (or join optional tours), and a guided Macau day trip on departure day. All transfers, airfare via Cebu Pacific, and breakfasts included.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 No meals

Manila → Hong Kong (Half Day City Tour)

  • Departure from Manila via Cebu Pacific
  • Arrival in Hong Kong
  • Enjoy a Half Day City Tour
  • Visit West Kowloon Cultural District (photo stop at M+ Museum and Hong Kong Palace Museum)
  • Photo stop at Victoria Harbour
  • Visit Jewellery Shop & Souvenir Shop
  • Visit Avenue of Stars
  • Visit Clock Tower in Tsim Sha Tsui
  • Hotel check-in at Cozi Oasis (4-Star)
Day 2 Breakfast

Hong Kong — Free Time or Optional Tour

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Free day — explore Hong Kong on your own, or join an optional tour (e.g. Disneyland, Ocean Park, Ngong Ping 360, Lantau Island)
Day 3 Breakfast

Hong Kong — Free Time or Optional Tour

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Another full free day — shopping, dim sum, or join an optional tour
Day 4 Breakfast & Lunch

Macau Day Trip → Manila

  • Breakfast at the hotel and check-out
  • Proceed to Macau
  • Enjoy Macau City Tour
  • Visit Ruins of St. Paul's, Senado Square, Na Tcha Temple, and A-Ma Temple
  • Photo stop at Nam Van Lake
  • Visit Souvenir Shop
  • Enjoy Casino at Venetian Macao
  • Transfer to airport & departure for Manila
