Great Singapore 2026-2027

4 Days / 3 Nights or 5 Days / 3 Nights  ·  via Cebu Pacific  ·  from ₱30,888

Package Overview

Singapore — the city-state that does everything well. Sky-high gardens, hawker-stall Michelin stars, futuristic architecture, and one of the world's easiest airports to land in. This package gets you there from Manila with Cebu Pacific, settles you into 3 nights at a Singapore hotel (twin sharing), and includes a guided city tour with photo stops at the icons.

Choose your travel window — 4 Days / 3 Nights or 5 Days / 3 Nights (same hotel nights, longer free-and-easy stay). Either way, you get airport transfers, daily breakfast, and a professional guide for the city tour. The free days are yours: shopping at Orchard, dim sum in Chinatown, sunset at Marina Bay, or a Universal Studios day trip (optional).

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 No meals

Manila → Singapore (Free & Easy)

  • Departure from Manila via Cebu Pacific
  • Arrival at Singapore Changi Airport
  • Private transfer to hotel, check-in
  • Free & Easy — explore around your hotel area
Day 2 Breakfast

Half-Day Singapore City Tour

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Proceed to City Tour with professional guide
  • Photo stops: Merlion, Kampong Glam, Thian Hock Keng Temple, Gardens by the Bay
  • Drive-by: Marina Bay Sands, Singapore Flyer, Parliament House, Cricket, National Gallery
  • Free & Easy in the afternoon
Day 3 Breakfast

Singapore — Free & Easy

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Free day — suggested: discover scenic views, walk around nearby spots, or enjoy shopping at nearby markets
  • Optional add-ons (on request): Universal Studios Singapore, S.E.A. Aquarium, Night Safari, Singapore Zoo
Day 4 Breakfast

Departure (4D3N) or Free & Easy (5D3N)

  • 4D3N variant: Check-out, transfer to airport, depart Singapore via 5J806 SINMNL 18:25–22:30, arrive Manila
  • 5D3N variant: Free & Easy throughout the day, proceed to airport in the evening
Day 5 (5D3N variant only) No meals

Singapore → Manila

  • Departure from Singapore via 5J804 SINMNL 01:50–05:45
  • Arrival in Manila
