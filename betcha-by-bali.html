Betcha by Bali WOW 2.0

5 Days / 3 Nights  ·  via Cebu Pacific  ·  Risata Bali Resort (4-Star)  ·  from ₱32,888

Package Overview

The Island of the Gods is calling. This 5-day, 3-night Bali package takes you straight from Manila into the heart of Indonesia's most iconic destination — sweeping rice terraces in Ubud, sacred sea temples at Tanah Lot, the towering Garuda Wisnu Kencana statue, and the volcanic vistas of Kintamani.

Stay 3 nights at the 4-star Risata Bali Resort (or similar class), enjoy full board meals across all touring days, and travel in comfort with a private coach, an English-speaking local guide, and a dedicated Filipino tour escort. Tours are fully loaded — no guesswork, no add-ons to worry about. Just show up and enjoy Bali.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Lunch & Dinner

Manila → Bali (Garuda Wisnu Kencana & Coffee Factory)

  • Departure from Manila via Cebu Pacific
  • Arrival in Bali
  • Visit Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK) Cultural Park
  • Visit Bali Coffee Factory
  • Hotel check-in at Risata Bali Resort (or similar 4-star class)
Day 2 Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Ubud & Kintamani Full-Day Tour

  • Visit UC Silver — traditional Balinese silver workshop
  • Visit Tampaksiring (Holy Spring Temple)
  • Visit Kintamani — panoramic views of Mount Batur volcano
  • Visit Ubud Rice Terrace (Tegalalang)
  • Visit Ubud Palace (Puri Saren Agung)
  • Visit Ubud Market
Day 3 Breakfast

Free & Easy

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Free day — discover scenic views, walk around nearby spots, or enjoy shopping at nearby markets
  • Optional add-on activities available on request (e.g. Bali swing, ATV, spa, additional tours)
Day 4 Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner

Temples of Bali — Mengwi, Ulun Danu & Tanah Lot

  • Visit Mengwi Royal Temple (Taman Ayun)
  • Visit Ulun Danu Beratan Temple — the iconic lakeside temple
  • Visit Tanah Lot — Bali's famous sea temple at sunset
  • Transfer to airport for return flight
Day 5 No meals

Bali → Manila

  • Departure from Bali
  • Arrival in Manila
