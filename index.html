About Us

A Boutique Gateway with a Personal Touch

My Destination Tours and Travel is a DOT-accredited boutique travel agency based in Antipolo City, Rizal. We believe travel should be seamless, personal, and memorable — from your first inquiry to the moment you arrive back home.

Our passionate team of travel consultants brings expertise, dedication, and genuine care to every booking. We partner with trusted airlines, hotels, and tour operators to deliver exceptional value at honest prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our Services

Quick answers about our travel services, accreditation, and booking process.

Where is My Destination Tours and Travel located?
Our office is located at 3F SM Center Antipolo Downtown, Marcos Highway, Brgy. Mayamot, Antipolo, Rizal 1870, Philippines. We are easily accessible from Marikina, Cainta, Taytay, Pasig, and the rest of Metro Manila. You can visit us in person, call us, or message us online.
Are you accredited and registered?
Yes. My Destination Tours and Travel is fully registered and accredited:
  • Department of Tourism (DOT) accreditation: RIVA-TTA-000191-2019
  • Philippine Travel Agencies Association (PTAA) member: RE-2024-335
  • DTI Registration No. 05051356
  • Member of Rizal Travel Agencies Association Inc.
We have been operating since November 26, 2018.
What services do you offer?
We provide a complete range of travel services:
  • Domestic and international tour packages
  • Flight bookings (with all major airlines)
  • Hotel reservations
  • Tourist visa assistance for Japan, Korea, USA, Canada, Schengen, Australia, New Zealand, India, and Turkey
  • Travel insurance
  • Land transfers and car rentals
  • Customized itineraries based on your budget and preferences
How do I book a trip with you?
It's simple. You can: (1) fill out our inquiry form on this website, (2) email us at sales@travelwithmyd.com, (3) call us at 8635-5022 or 0933-818-5552, or (4) message us on Facebook. Our team will respond within 24 hours with options, pricing, and next steps.
Do you handle visa applications?
Yes. We provide expert visa consultancy and assistance for tourist visa applications to Japan, Korea, USA (B1/B2), Canada (and ETA), Schengen countries, Australia, New Zealand, India, and Turkey. Our service includes document review, application completion, appointment scheduling, and interview coaching.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit/debit card payments, GCash, and cash payments at our Antipolo office. For card payments, we use the Card Authorization Form to securely process your transaction. Payment terms vary by service — please ask your travel consultant for details.
Can travel itineraries be customized?
Absolutely. While we have set tour packages available, we specialize in customizing itineraries to match your individual needs, interests, group size, and budget. Just share what you have in mind and we'll build a personalized plan.
