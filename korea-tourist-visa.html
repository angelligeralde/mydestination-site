Visa Consultancy

Expert Korea Tourist Visa Assistance

From document review to KVAC submission, our team handles your Korea Tourist Visa application end-to-end with one transparent all-in fee.

Your Trusted Partner for Korea Visa Applications

Dreaming of a trip to Seoul, Busan, or Jeju Island? The Republic of Korea has become one of the top destinations for Filipino travelers, but the visa process requires careful preparation.

That's where we come in.

Our team takes care of every step of your Korea Tourist Visa application — from form completion to compiling supporting documents and submitting them through the Korea Visa Application Center (KVAC). For most applicants, no embassy interview is required, but documents must be flawless.

Whether you're a first-time applicant or have been denied previously, we'll prepare your application with the attention to detail it deserves.

Processing Time: Visa processing times vary by embassy/consulate and season. Please contact our agents for the most current processing time estimate for your application.

Our Korea Visa Assistance Service

We assist you throughout the key steps of your Korea visa application process.

1

Application Form Assistance

The Korea visa application form must be completed accurately and submitted with the right supporting documents.

We will:

  • Guide you through every section of the application form
  • Ensure information matches your supporting documents
  • Review for completeness and accuracy
  • Prepare a properly-organized submission packet
2

Document Preparation & Review

Korea requires comprehensive financial proof, employment documentation, and travel plans.

We will:

  • Provide the complete document checklist
  • Review your bank statements and ITR
  • Help you build a credible travel itinerary
  • Verify documents meet embassy standards
3

KVAC Submission & Appointment

All applications go through the Korea Visa Application Center (KVAC) at Aseana City, Parañaque.

We will:

  • Schedule your KVAC appointment
  • Brief you on submission-day procedures
  • Coordinate biometrics capture if required
  • Track your application until release
4

Ongoing Support & Questions

We're here to answer your questions before, during, and after submission.

We will:

  • Clarify visa requirements and exceptions
  • Help frequent travelers with multiple-entry options
  • Guide first-time applicants step by step
  • Support you through approval and passport pickup

Who Should Use Our Visa Assistance Service?

  • First-time Korea visa applicants
  • Travelers who want guidance through the process
  • Applicants who want to avoid mistakes in their forms
  • Anyone who wants extra preparation and document review
